About Me

Hello! I'm a freelancer based in Wevelgem, Belgium, specializing in providing high-quality lighting, laser, and video technical services to businesses within the event industry. I primarily work with **Chamsys MQ500M+** and **MA3** lighting consoles, bringing advanced control and precision to every lighting design and operation.

For laser projects, I utilize **Pangolin Beyond Ultimate** software to create stunning and safe laser displays. My pre-visualization capabilities are powered by **Vectorworks Designer** and **Capture**, allowing for detailed planning and realistic renderings of event setups. For video projects, I mostly use **Resolume Arena** to deliver dynamic and impactful visual content.

With a passion for creating impactful visual experiences, I bring extensive experience and a meticulous approach to every project. My focus is on understanding the unique needs of each event and collaborating closely with clients to deliver solutions that enhance atmosphere, highlight key elements, and ensure seamless technical execution. I am dedicated to professionalism, reliability, and bringing your event's vision to light.
