About Me

Hello! I'm a freelancer based in Wevelgem, Belgium, specialising in providing high-quality lighting, laser, and video technical services to businesses within the event industry. I primarily work with Chamsys MQ500M+ and MA3 lighting consoles, bringing advanced control and precision to every lighting design and operation.

For laser projects, I utilize Pangolin Beyond Ultimate software to create stunning and safe laser displays. My pre-visualisation capabilities are powered by Vectorworks Designer and Capture, allowing for detailed planning and realistic renderings. For video projects, I use Resolume Arena to deliver dynamic visual content.

With a passion for creating impactful visual experiences, I bring extensive experience and a meticulous approach to every project. My focus is on understanding the unique needs of each event and collaborating closely with clients to deliver solutions that enhance atmosphere and ensure seamless technical execution.
